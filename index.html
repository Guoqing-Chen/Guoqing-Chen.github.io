Biography

I received the M.Sc. degree at Xihua university concerning the project "Research on the role of green finance in upgrading industrial structure from the perspective of sustainable development" in June 2019, supervised by Prof. Yun-an Long. Now I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Statistics, Mahasarakham University, Thailand since 2022, supervised by Prof. Piyapatr Busababodhin.

My research interest Combination forecast, Evolutionary Game Theory, Grey incidence analysis and Difference-in-Differences model (DID).
